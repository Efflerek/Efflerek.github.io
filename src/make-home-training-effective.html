Is At-Home EMS Training Your Fit?

Before embarking on your EMS journey, it's crucial to consider your health profile. EMS training is not recommended if you have a pacemaker, cardiovascular conditions, or an increased risk of thrombosis. Pregnant women should also explore alternative training methods.

Safety and Beyond: Navigating EMS Training

In a nutshell, the electrical currents encountered in Easy Motion Skin are entirely harmless. The nervous system mechanism underpinning EMS mirrors that of your body, differing only in the externally applied electric signal that triggers muscle contractions. While EMS training is safe, specific contraindications warrant consultation with a medical professional before initiation.
